OCUA Spirit Rating Guidelines

These guidelines are designed to help teams determine spirit rating.

Get your team to vote/decide on a spirit rating, and then submit your score and spirit rating via the online system. Few teams are going to nicely fit into the slots below, so use this chart as a guideline only.

8 - 10
  • All the players on my team enjoyed playing with all of the players on the opposing team.
  • My opponents showed up on time.
  • My opponents exhibited a great level of sportsmanship throughout the game.
  • My opponents were knowledgeable about the rules, or were receptive to learning them.
  • We even loved their cheer.
You Should...
  • invite them to join you for a beer.

6 - 8
  • All the players on my team enjoyed playing against almost all of the players on the opposing team.
  • My opponents showed up on time
  • My opponents exhibited a good level of sportsmanship throughout the game.
  • My opponents were knowledgeable about the rules, or were receptive to learning them.
You Should...
  • Talk to the other captain. Relay any incidents and/or the name(s) of the problem individual(s) if any.

4 - 6
  • Some of the players on my team enjoyed playing against some of the players on the opposing team.
  • My opponents showed up on time
  • My opponents exhibited some sportsmanship throughout the game.
  • My opponents were fairly knowledgeable about the rules.
You Should...
  • Talk to the other captain and discuss the game.

2 - 4
  • Very few of the players on my team enjoyed playing against anyone on the opposing team.
  • We had to wait for our opponents to have enough players to field a team. They were not on time.
  • There was an overall lack of sportsmanship exhibited by the opponents.
  • My opponents did not know the rules and did not want to learn them.
You Should...
  • Discuss the matter with the other captain and the league coordinator.
  • Get your cleats on, stand on the line, and start counting a point for every five minutes your team waits after the official start time.

1 - 2
  • None of the players on my team enjoyed playing against the opposing team.
  • They were not on time.
  • There was an overall lack of sportsmanship exhibited by the opponents.
  • My opponents did not know the rules and did not want to learn them.
You Should...
  • Talk to the league coordinator.
  • Get your cleats on, stand on the line, and start counting a point for every five minutes your team waits after the official start time.

0
  • None of the players on my team enjoyed playing against the opposing team.
  • My opponents were not on time and were not able to field a team at all.
  • My opponents displayed no sportsmanship and initiated several instances of dangerous play.
  • My opponents did not know the rules and did not want to learn them.
  • They were the worst-spirited team I have ever played against.
You Should...
  • Talk to the league coordinator.
  • Get your cleats on, stand on the line, and start counting a point for every five minutes your team waits after the official start time, to a maximum of 6 points.
